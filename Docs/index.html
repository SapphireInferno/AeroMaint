Examining a client's SAP ERP implementation
to see how they can fly simple.

Asset management is an important functional area for organizations involved in
air operations. Aircraft are valuable assets, and critically important to these organization's
ability to fulfil their mandates. They're costly to procure, operate, and maintain yet need to
be available when demands for their services are placed.

Why Change

The organization studied performs asset management functions for aircraft using SAP ERP. While SAP ERP includes a wide range of support for the required business processes, there are numerous areas where standard SAP ERP functionality becomes cumbersome when applied against such complex assets.

What Challenges

Extensively manual processes to maintain data, Akward user interfaces with functionality spread across many individual screens, Standard SAP ERP functionality not fully configured for end-user usage

Which Technogies

Using Archi to model the current and future states of the organization, SAP ERP 'Support to Flight Operations', SAP Multiresource Scheduling, and SAP FIORI, UI5, and Netweaver Gateway all feature in the proposed future state of the application architecture.
