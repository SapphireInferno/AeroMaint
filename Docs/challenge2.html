Automated update of Measurement Point Annual Estimates

Challenge Two

The Importance of Annual Estimates

In Performance-Based Maintenance, we monitor some variables or measurement points. Every measurement point has an annual estimate, which is used by SAP Enterprise Asset Management (EAM) to schedule maintenance tasks.

Updating annual estimates is a tedious process, since hundreds or even thousands of annual estimates need to be updated one by one. Nobody has the time -or the will, for that matter- to do it and, consequently, this important task has not been done in a while, leading to innacurate maintenance scheduling.

To simplify updating annual estimates, our solution is to develop a custom application to massively update key measurement points based on aircraft's technical structure hierarchies (functional locations, equipment and measurement points).

Motivation Layer

The more accurate the annual estimates are, the more reliable are the predicted dates of maintenance calculated by the ERP on the long term. Accurate scheduling contributes to better aircraft availability.

Business Layer

Demand data (challenge 1) and annual estimates are combined to plan maintenance, authorize the maximum aircraft utilization by type for a year and forecast individual aircraft usage.
