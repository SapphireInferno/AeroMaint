Lack of Demand Data

Challenge One

Lack of Demand Data

In order to program the future work, the Maintenance department needs to know the future demand of aircraft. Right now, dynamically changing mission details are resident in a system accessible only to Operations Planners and delivered to Maintenance personnel periodically using static MS Office documents, which are used to plan aircraft type utilization and maintenance.

However, to supply aircrafts for flights, Maintenance personnel require up-to-date knowledge of demand data. Therefore, instead of using Excel files, our proposed solution is to configure native SAP ECC DFPS Support to Flight Operations ‘Flight Order’. Operations Planners will maintain relevant details of Missions within the Flight Orders which will be accessible to Maintenance personnel. Flight Orders can reference specific aircraft (Master Equipment) and link to Maintenance Orders for the maintenance required to prepare the aircraft for a flight.

Motivation Layer

The maintenance personnel requires demand data (upcoming missions details) to minimize mission failures and cancellations due to unavailability of aircraft.
