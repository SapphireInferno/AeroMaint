Challenge Five

Meet Gary

This challenge is related to user experience, with a focus on data reporting

Gary is our Maintenance Manager. His main responsibilities are:

  • • Control all maintenance at a squadron:
    - manages 15~20 aircraft
    - oversees 250~300 technicians, supervisors, planners, etc.
  • • Supply aircraft to Operations group for missions
Along the way, he experiences the following frustrations:
  • • Information he needs is scattered across several traditional SAP ERP modules:
    - Flight Orders
    - Maintenance Notifications / Orders
    - Maintenance Order Capacity Requirements
    - Work Center / Capacities Available

Two Worlds

Scattered Information

Convergence of Information

Simultaneous display of aircraft demand and supply through flight and maintenance orders
