Bad user experience for Shift Supervisors

Challenge Four

Meet Albert

In solving user experience (UX)-related problems, we begin by knowing our persona.

Albert is our Maintenance Shift Supervisor. His main responsibilities are:

  • a.) To control the maintenance performed by his shift
  • b.) Oversee participation of shift personnel in non-maintenance activities
  • c.) Report maintenance labor capacity to the planner
Along the way, he experiences the following frustrations:
  • a.) Heavy reliance on whiteboards and post-it’s which are crude forms of documentation and may result in inaccuracies and inconsistencies
  • b.) Existing user interfaces does not have context: these does not provide him the very information that he needs
  • c.) Complex navigation structures not only working his way through the system cumbersome but adds overhead time in accomplishing his job

Proposed UI5 based apps

Retrieve Individual Schedule

The Retrieve Individual Schedule app provides a calendar view of the personnel’s availability as indicated by three label types: training, leave and others This app is useful for planning schedules and operations ahead of time.

View Personnel Profile

The View Personnel Profile is designed as a one stop viewpoint for personnel’s salient information as required for the shift supervisor’s decision making Personnel information include: - Personal Data – for basic information - Planned Working Time – for the work schedule - Qualifications – for skills, trainings, certifications - Authorizations – for granted permissions to use those acquired skills Personnel can be filtered by name and parent and child work centers And this sidebar is uniform across all applications.

Manage Maintenance Tasks

Tasks are shown and filtered by parent and child work centers and this app enables the shift supervisor to assign tasks and associated time elements such as expected work duration.

Shift Planning Board

This app is designed for planning the shift activities. Personnel, their operations and availabilities are represented in timeline view.
